Notes & Caveats

  • Input‑only pins (GPIO34–GPIO39) have no internal pull‑ups/downs; use external resistors if needed. They are excellent for ADC1 but cannot drive outputs.
  • ADC2 channels (GPIOs 0, 2, 4, 12–15, 25–27) are shared with Wi‑Fi. Avoid analog reads on ADC2 while Wi‑Fi is active.
  • Strapping pins (GPIO0, GPIO2, GPIO5, GPIO12/MTDI, GPIO15/MTDO) are sampled on reset to select boot mode and SDIO options; keep their levels benign at power‑up.
  • Flash/PSRAM pins (GPIO6–GPIO11) are internally connected; do not use them for external I/O on the WROVER module.
  • UART0 (TXD0/RXD0) is used for boot logs by default; you can repurpose after boot if logs are disabled.
